Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a review?

To submit a review, simply sign in to your account and click on the "Write a Review" button. Follow the prompts to share your travel experience.

What is your response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please contact us by phone.

Can I become a partner?

Yes! We're always looking for partnerships with travel businesses. Please select "Partnership Opportunities" in the contact form above.

How can I report an issue?

If you encounter any issues, please use our contact form and select "Technical Support" as the subject. Our team will assist you promptly.
