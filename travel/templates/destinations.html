Travel Tips

Flight Booking

  • Book flights 3-4 months in advance for best deals
  • Use incognito mode when searching for flights
  • Consider flexible dates for better prices

Accommodation

  • Read recent reviews before booking
  • Check the location on maps
  • Compare prices across multiple platforms

Money Saving

  • Use local transportation instead of taxis
  • Cook some meals instead of eating out
  • Visit free attractions and museums

Navigation

  • Download offline maps before your trip
  • Learn basic local phrases
  • Keep your hotel's business card

Safety Tips

  • Keep important documents backed up
  • Get travel insurance
  • Stay aware of your surroundings

Photography

  • Research photo spots beforehand
  • Shoot during golden hours
  • Back up your photos regularly
